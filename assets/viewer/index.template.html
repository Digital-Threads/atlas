How to read this map
Every focused view follows one rule. The left side uses or enters the selected element. The right side is what it calls, depends on, changes, or owns. Click any box to make it the new center.
Boxes are parts of the system. The color stripe shows what kind: route, service, data, event… Click a box — Atlas explains what it is and what it touches.
Arrows show who acts on whom. The word on the line says what happens: “calls”, “publishes”, “reads”…
Solid line = happens right away (the caller waits). Moving dashed line = happens later — a message through Kafka or a queue. Green = data, red = an external service.
Numbers show the order. Open a route or a Kafka topic and follow 1 → 2 → 3 to see how the process starts, what it does, and where it ends.
